## Connect With Us in Person & Online

NorthLite Solar regularly hosts and participates in clean energy conferences, diaspora investment roundtables, and local community workshops. Join us to learn more about our commercial engineering projects, zero-investment PPA structures, and off-grid community initiatives.

---

### Upcoming Featured Events

#### 1. USA-Ghana Diaspora Solar Investment Roundtable
*   **Location**: New York City, NY (And streamed online)
*   **Date**: October 14, 2026
*   **Focus**: A dedicated session for the West African diaspora living in the United States. We will detail how to securely finance, monitor, and deploy solar PV systems for family properties and businesses back home in Ghana.
*   **Keynote**: *Frank Adabre Akasoba (CEO)* on "Building Transnational Solar Infrastructure: Connecting Capital and Impact."

#### 2. C&I Solar Strategy Webinar: Transitioning Heavy Industry
*   **Location**: Virtual Event (Zoom Link provided upon registration)
*   **Date**: November 5, 2026
*   **Focus**: Designed for commercial real estate developers, factory managers, and agricultural directors. Our engineering team will walk through load profiling, peak-shaving mechanics, and structured lease-to-own models that preserve business capital.

#### 3. Off-Grid Community Empowerment Seminar
*   **Location**: Bolgatanga Regional Office, Upper East Region, Ghana
*   **Date**: December 10, 2026
*   **Focus**: A local outreach event celebrating the commissioning of our latest community solar water pump installation. We will hold open training sessions for local technicians on safety, basic maintenance, and grid troubleshooting.
