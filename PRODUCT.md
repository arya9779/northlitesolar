# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Commercial and industrial (C&I) facility owners, diaspora investors, agricultural/humanitarian program sponsors, and energy investment SPV partners looking for reliable, high-performance clean energy infrastructure. They value technical clarity, engineering competence, and investment security over flashy marketing.

## Product Purpose
NorthLite Solar delivers premium transcontinental clean energy infrastructure (bridging Maryland, US and Ghana). The site exists to build trust, showcase engineering capability, explain SPV finance structures, and display the Diaspora Solar and Smart SERU programs. Success is when visitors see a highly structured, reliable clean energy enterprise and reach out for partnerships or installations.

## Brand Personality
Structured, sustainable, authoritative. Speaks with an engineering-led, expert voice. The tone is clean, technical, precise, and professional.

## Anti-references
- "AI native" visual slop: floating canvas particles, neon cyan/purple/gold gradients, glassmorphic cards, glowing borders.
- SaaS marketing clichés: identical-card grid lists, hero-metric big number templates with tiny labels, sparklines, and generic "boost your productivity" copy.
- Unnecessary visual decorations or ornament for ornament's sake.

## Design Principles
1. **Engineering-Led Structure**: The layout must feel structured and technically sound. Use clean lines, clear grids, and precise alignment.
2. **Breathing Room & Restraint**: Avoid clutter. Use generous whitespace to focus attention on typography, structural grids, and project assets.
3. **Purposeful Accents**: Limit colors and indicators. Keep the solar gold accent to 10% or less, using it only to draw attention to critical actions or status.
4. **Authoritative Copy**: Speak directly. Avoid hyperbole or marketing fluff. Present specifications, structural diagrams, and project details with technical accuracy.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- WCAG 2.1 AA contrast levels for text and interactive controls.
- Keyboard navigable controls with highly visible, crisp focus borders.
- Minimal motion, respecting `prefers-reduced-motion` at all levels.
